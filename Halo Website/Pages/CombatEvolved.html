Review

Halo: Combat Evolved Anniversary is an outstanding first-person shooter that remains a joy to play nearly a decade after its original release. This remastered version looks and plays great, but audio bugs and downgraded Classic visuals sully the experience.

Summary

Halo: Combat Evolved is a first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie and published by Microsoft Game Studios. ... Players battle various aliens as they attempt to uncover the secrets of the eponymous Halo, a ring-shaped artificial world. Bungie began the development of what would eventually become Halo in 1997.
